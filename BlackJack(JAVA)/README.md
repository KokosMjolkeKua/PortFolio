BLACKJACK IN JAVA:

Started making this project in the christmas of 2025, started with the deck and card. Getting individual
values for cards and made a Deck that creates the 52 cards in a List. Then made the dealer, dealing the cards
i also made player.

GAME RULES:
Card Values
2-10: Face value.
J, Q, K: 10 points each.
Ace (A): 1 or 11 points (player's choice).

Gameplay
Betting: Place chips in the designated area.
Dealing: Players get two cards face up; the dealer gets one face up, one face down.

Player's Turn:
Hit: Ask for another card (tap table).
Stand: Keep your current hand (wave hand).
Bust: If your total exceeds 21, you lose immediately.
Blackjack: An Ace and a 10-value card (21 on first two cards).
Dealer's Turn: Dealer reveals their face-down card and hits
until their total is 17 or more (rules vary slightly).
